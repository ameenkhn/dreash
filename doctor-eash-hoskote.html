Dr Eash Hoskote

Dr. Eash Hoskote is a senior Consultant Pediatrician and Neonatologist with over 25 years of experience in comprehensive child healthcare. His clinical practice focuses on newborn and neonatal care, preventive pediatrics, management of childhood illnesses, immunizations, growth and nutrition monitoring, and adolescent health, ensuring continuity of care from birth through adolescence.

About Doctor

Dr. Eash Hoskote is a highly respected Consultant Pediatrician and Neonatologist with over 25 years of extensive clinical and leadership experience in pediatric and maternal healthcare. He combines deep medical expertise with a strong patient-first philosophy, ensuring that every child receives ethical, evidence-based, and development-focused care tailored to individual needs.


An alumnus of Mysore Medical College, Dr. Eash holds an MBBS, a Diploma in Child Health (RGUHS), and a Postgraduate qualification in Paediatric Nutrition from Boston University. He is currently enhancing his healthcare leadership and administrative expertise through the General Management for Hospital Executives (GMHE) program at IIM Bangalore, reflecting his commitment to clinical excellence and healthcare system strengthening.


Dr. Eash has extensive hands-on experience in managing newborns, preterm and low-birth-weight infants, acute and chronic childhood infections, allergies, asthma, growth and nutritional disorders, developmental delays, and behavioral concerns. He is particularly known for the early identification of developmental issues and for guiding families through long-term growth, nutrition, and preventive healthcare strategies.


Beyond clinical practice, Dr. Eash has played a pivotal role in shaping pediatric healthcare delivery systems. As Medical and Zonal Director at Ovum Hospitals, Bengaluru, he led multidisciplinary teams across NICU, PICU, pediatric surgery, developmental pediatrics, and maternity services, contributing significantly to improved care standards, operational efficiency, and patient outcomes.


A committed academician and healthcare leader, Dr. Eash serves as a senior office bearer within the Indian Academy of Pediatrics, has chaired and contributed to multiple national and state pediatric conferences, and regularly participates as faculty at high-level medical forums. His approach to medicine emphasizes ethical practice, transparent communication with parents, preventive care, and the nurturing of long-term child health and well-being.

Experience in Bangalore

Dr Hoskote has been serving the pediatric community in Bangalore – South since 2000. Over these years, he has worked in multiple hospitals as a consultant pediatrician and neonatologist, collaborating closely with obstetricians, neonatologists, nurses, and allied health professionals. His long-standing presence in the region has allowed him to follow many children from birth through their teenage years, building enduring relationships with families and gaining a deep understanding of local health needs.
