Comprehensive Pediatric & Neonatal Care

Under the leadership of Dr. Eash Hoskote, our pediatric and neonatal services focus on providing safe, evidence-based, and compassionate care for newborns, infants, children, and adolescents. The approach emphasizes early diagnosis, preventive healthcare, growth and developmental monitoring, and clear guidance for parents—ensuring that every child receives age-appropriate, holistic medical care in a supportive environment.

How Our Pediatric & Neonatal Care Services Work

Dr. Eash Hoskote provides comprehensive pediatric and neonatal care through a structured, family-centered approach. Services include newborn and neonatal assessments, care of preterm and low-birth-weight infants, routine pediatric consultations, childhood illness management, immunizations, growth and developmental monitoring, nutrition guidance, and adolescent health support. Each care plan is evidence-based, carefully monitored, and personalized to the child's age, developmental stage, and medical needs.

Comprehensive Pediatric & Neonatal Services Working Together

Neonatal Intensive Care (NICU)

Advanced care for premature and high-risk newborns including stabilization, monitoring, and respiratory support under expert neonatology supervision.

Pediatric Intensive Care (PICU)

Specialized intensive care for critically ill children with round-the-clock monitoring and multidisciplinary pediatric support.

Inpatient Pediatric Care

Comfortable, child-friendly inpatient facilities supported by trained pediatric nurses and family-centered care practices.

Pediatric Pharmacy

In-house pharmacy stocked with child-safe medications, neonatal formulations, vaccines, and emergency pediatric drugs.

Pediatric Laboratory Services

Accurate and timely diagnostic services including neonatal screening, infection markers, metabolic tests, and routine pediatric investigations.

Pediatric Imaging & Diagnostics

Child-safe imaging services including X-rays and ultrasound, supporting accurate diagnosis with minimal discomfort.

24×7 Pediatric Emergency Care

Immediate emergency care for newborns and children, including acute illness, respiratory distress, and trauma.

Child Nutrition & Growth Support

Personalized nutrition counselling, growth monitoring, and feeding guidance for infants, toddlers, and adolescents.
